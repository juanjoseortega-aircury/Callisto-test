CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

About Callisto:

Callisto is a rapidly growing nonprofit tech startup that has created an online sexual assault reporting and documentation system that helps identify serial sexual predators. The system launched 2.5 years ago on 2 pilot college campuses, and has since grown to 12 colleges and an entertainment industry pilot. Reporting on participating campuses has doubled, assaults have been reported 3 times faster, and 15% of victims have matched with another victim of the same assailant (meaning they were informed that they weren't the only one and connected to the same investigator or legal advisor). In the next year, we will be rapidly expanding to address sexual assault and coercion in multiple industries, connecting victims of the same predator together and with legal support.

About The Position:

Callisto is seeking a Chief Operating Officer to become the third member of our executive team (joining a CEO and a Chief Product Officer). The COO will oversee a period of rapid growth, ensuring team coordination, seamless flow of information, legal and financial compliance, and a healthy culture. This position is located in San Francisco.

Responsibilities:
  • Strategy: Help ensure strategic and realistic long-term planning informed by the needs of the team, and break down long-term targets into short-term objectives
  • Operations: Ensure that the internal operations of the organization run smoothly and that processes are put in place to deliver performance and achieve goals
  • People: Oversee recruiting and HR, ensuring that a healthy team culture is created and maintained
You Have:
  • Experience successfully scaling companies from less than 15 to over 40
  • A knowledge of or strong desire to learn about tech startup and/or nonprofit operations
  • A love of management and processes, but a practical approach to both (sets up processes that will integrate well and be used, not process for process sake)
  • Obsessive attention to detail tempered by extraordinary prioritization skills - you notice when things aren’t right but can also backburner when appropriate
We'll Give You
  • Health, vision and dental insurance
  • Paid holidays, vacation, and sick leave
  • Salary commensurate with experience
  • A professional development budget and guidance to help your career grow as our organization grows
To apply, please send the following to jobs@projectcallisto.org with the subject line "COO":
  • Resume
  • Cover Letter
