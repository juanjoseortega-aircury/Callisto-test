The Problem

Sexual assault and harassment are far too common and often never reported. The process of reporting can feel isolating — or worse, retraumatizing — not to mention comes with its own set of personal and professional risks.

Callisto is changing the equation. Our technology empowers victims, giving them options and allowing them to disclose in a way that feels safe. Our unique matching system securely connects victims of the same perpetrator to identify repeat offenders.

Our Solutions

Currently in place across 13 partner institutions, Callisto Campus provides victims with trauma-sensitive options for reporting sexual assault. Callisto is expanding in 2018 to address sexual assault, harassment, and coercion in professional industries.

ORIGNAL

Callisto Campus

Callisto Campus is designed to detect repeat perpetrators and empower victims to make the reporting decision that feels right for them.

Our flagship online system, Callisto Campus, provides survivors with three options:

WRITE

Save time-stamped written records of a sexual assault

MATCH

Report only if another victim names the same perpetrator

REPORT

Report directly to campus administrators, without waiting for a match

NEW

Callisto Expansion

In 2018 we are expanding our reporting platform into professional environments. In this version of Callisto, we are adapting our signature matching system to empower victims beyond college campuses:

MATCH

Under the Callisto "Matching Escrow," victims input the identity of their perpetrators under the precondition that, if a match is found, a Callisto Options Counselor will reach out to each victim individually.

OPTIONS COUNSELING

A Callisto Options Counselor will advise victims on their choices following a match, including reporting — only if this is the right route for them.
